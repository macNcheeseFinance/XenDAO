What is Xen DAO?

Xen DAO offers 2 tools that allow to mass claim&mint XEN with ease. You can mint up to 111 wallets with a single click.
Both tools are non-custodial. Only you have the access to your tokens.

Benefits of using the Tool

In addition to claiming XEN, you will also earn Xen DAO(XD) token.

Economic Model

The DAO collects a small fee from each claimed wallet. 100% of the fees collected are distributed to Xen DAO(XD) stakers.

Xen DAO Staking

Xen DAO holders can stake their tokens & earn rewards from fees in native tokens(ETH/BNB/MATIC/...).

Xen DAO Tools

1.) Batch Minter: Generates wallets on-chain
(Pros: Most simple with 1 click, gas is charged at time of transaction. Cons: Costs up to 50% more in gas fees)

2.) Wallet Minter: Generates wallets in-browser
(Cons: More complicated, have to estimate gas fee which varies, Have to manually store wallets and keys. Pros: Uses less gas )

TLDR: Custom wallet minter is cheaper(costs less gas), batch contract minter is more convenient.


Xen DAO Tokenomics

Xen DAO(XD) token can only be minted until 15th of January 2023. Daily inflation starts reducing from day 14 onwards. 0 inflation is reached on 15th January 2023

No Premine

Xen DAO has no "premine". Supply starts at 0.

Referrals earn Extra

You can also referr other users and earn 20% of their XD reward. Invitee receives a 25% XD bonus on their mint. (Click to referr)

Xen DAO Long Term Value

Xen DAO is a smart contract that allows mass XEN minting and the distribution of collected rewards to XD stakers. There is no roadmap and no future development.
The main purpose of Xen DAO is to create a safe batch minting environment with extra rewards. It is not meant to serve as an investment. Beware that the XEN token itself is hyperinflationary which could lead to tokens becoming worthless.

Xen DAO Security

Xen DAO is an experimental software. Source code can be viewed on Github. While it's most likely safe and secure, there have been no audits from a professional auditing firm. Notice that the tool is still in beta and unexpected bugs could occur. Use at your own risk.
